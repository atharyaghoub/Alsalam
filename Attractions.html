Mobirise

Wellington (Te Whanga-nui-a-Tara)

 Zealandia - Protected natural area, you will see native birds.
Hobbit Movie Tour - Take a trip to visit Weta Workshop, where movie magic is made.
City Tour - From harbour to hills and all the fascinating places in between, discover Wellington's charm and scenic highlights.

Auckalnd (Tāmaki-makau-rau)

SkyTower - View the city from 220 metres high above street level.
Harbour Cruse - Cruise the sparkling waters of Auckland’s beautiful Waitemata Harbour, and learn about the city landmarks.
City Tour - This half day tour takes you around the central city, experiencing many of its best and most scenic sights.

Taupo (Taupō-nui-a Tia)

Rapids Jet - Taupo whitewater Jet boat Experience. High speed jumps and turns over Whitewater Rapids.
Tongariro River Rafting - This half-day Tongariro River journey is one of the best rafting adventures in NZ.
Chris Jolly Outdoors Scenic Cruises - Cruise to the famous Mine Bay Maori Rock Carvings and around the picturesque bays of Lake Taupo.

Rotorua (Rotorua-nui-a-Kahu Matamōmoe) 

Mitai Maori Village - Mitai Maori Village evening experience offers an authentic traditional Maori experience
Agrodome Farm Show - Working farm with a popular farm show, plus a tour, animal interaction area & wool demonstrations.
Adventure Playground – Quad Bike riding and Clay Bird Shooting

Glacier (Waiparahoaka)

International Antarctic Centre - You’ll visit ‘Antarctica’ the snow and ice experience, survive an Antarctic Storm, learn about life in modern day Antarctica and Scott Base, and hang out with Little Blue Penguins
Glacier Heli Hike - You'll enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding mountains, and with plenty of photo opportunities, and the chance to learn more about this magnificent glacier,

Kaikoura (Te Ahi-kai-kōura-a-Tama-ki-te-rangi)

Whale Watch - Unique experience and the sightings vary. Giant sperm whales are the stars of the show and year-round residents.
Encounter Kaikoura - Take a dolphin swimming tour or simply watch dolphins from your purpose build boat off the coast of Kaikoura, one of the world’s best locations for dolphins.

Queenstown (Tahuna)

Shotover Jet - Take a unique breathtaking ride through dramatic and narrow canyons, and hold on tight for Queenstown’s only exhilarating full 360° spins and beyond.
Skyline Queenstown - Take a thrilling ride enjoyed by all ages and abilities.
Kawarau Bungy - World's first commercially operated Bungy Jumping Site, off the historic Kawarau Bridge.

Nelson (Whakatu)

World of WearableArt & Classic Cars Museum - The World of WearableArt and Classic Cars Museum is an iconic ‘must see’ visitor attraction that celebrates two distinctly different passions.
The Great Day Out - Travel in style and comfort to Tonga Island Marine Reserve. Pause to view the antics at Tonga Island Seal Colony then continue along the remote northern beaches where nikau palms and native Rata feature in the rainforest.
